Introduction to the News Center

The news center in Omnia is the place to go when you want to find and read all published news articles. If you are a news author, you go to the news center to create a new news article.

Note! In the News Center, all news articles are always available for all users. Targeting has no effect in the News Center.

The following Omnia Controls are related to the news center:

  • Create News Article
  • Latest News
  • Likes and Comments
  • News Archive
  • Related News
